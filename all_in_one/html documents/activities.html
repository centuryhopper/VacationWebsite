Activities at Atlantic Trails

Hiking
Atlantic Trails Resort has 15 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes. The table below lists some of our guided hikes and their departure times from the main office. Sign-up in the office for any or all of the hikes during your stay at Atlantic Trails. Our guides will point out many interesting features and landmarks during your hike.
